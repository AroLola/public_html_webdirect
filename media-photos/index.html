NABIRM GLOBAL

NABIRM management Team was beyond honored to meet with former President and Founding Father of Namibia, His Excellency, Dr. Sam Nujoma. We were accompanied by a special delegation from the Cabinet of the President of Sierra Leone, His Excellency, Ernest Bai Koroma. The Sierra Leonean delegation was sent to Namibia to support the NABIRM launch.
